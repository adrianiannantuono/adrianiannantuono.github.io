          about
          
        
      

      

        
        
      

      

        
          
            Founded in 2018 by Christian Iannantuono, tuono offers design and fabrication services
            across a variety of fields. It is a place where his passions of architecture, design,
            construction, fabrication, and the digital worlds meet in a specialized yet diversified
            and professional company. At tuono, we believe that design makes a difference. We strive
            to innovate, create, and deploy a range of solutions for your needs, that's our promise
            to you. It's not only your needs that keep us going, we are a group of passionate people
            aiming to create a movement.
